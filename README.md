# My Digital Resume

My Digital Resume is a dynamic web application designed to showcase employee information in an interactive and engaging format.

## Deployment

The application is live and accessible at [akashktr.pythonanywhere.com](https://akashktr.pythonanywhere.com).

## Features

- **Personal Information:** View details about the employee, including contact information and a downloadable PDF resume file.

- **Contact Form:** Easily connect with the employee by submitting a contact form to share your details.

- **Career Perspective:** Gain insights into the employee's career journey, skills, and aspirations.

- **Projects:** Explore the projects the employee has worked on, along with relevant details and achievements.

- **Experiences:** Dive into the employee's professional experiences, including roles, responsibilities, and accomplishments.

## Usage

To access the application and explore the features:

1. Visit [akashktr.pythonanywhere.com](https://akashktr.pythonanywhere.com) in your web browser.

2. Navigate through the different sections to learn more about the employee's background and skills.

3. Download the PDF resume for a comprehensive overview.

4. Use the provided contact form to connect with the employee and share your details.
